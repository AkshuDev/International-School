Welcome to InterNational School

ABOUT US

At InterNational School, we provide a supportive and inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, and personal growth. Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to succeed in an ever-changing world. We strive to make learning a joyful and engaging experience with a team of experienced educators and a commitment to innovation. Our school community is built on respect, empathy, and responsibility. We encourage students to take an active role in their educational journey and aim to inspire a lifelong love of learning. By working together, we help our students thrive and reach their full potential.

At InterNational School, we empower students to become confident, compassionate, and responsible individuals through academic excellence, creativity, and social growth. We deliver high-quality education tailored to each student's needs, inspiring a love of learning that lasts a lifetime. We will ensure that our students develop both the skills that a sound education provides and the competencies essential for success and leadership in the emerging creative economy.
At InterNational School, we believe that every child is unique, with their own interests and strengths. We strive to nurture these individual talents, whether in academics, sports, or other areas, to help our students grow into confident and well-rounded individuals. By embracing this diverse and inclusive approach, we aim to empower our students to become creative problem-solvers, responsible citizens, and future leaders who make a positive impact in the world.
Our plan to achieve our vision focuses on three key areas: Academic Excellence, where we will provide a rigorous and engaging curriculum with personalized learning experiences; Personalized Development, where we will offer extracurricular activities, sports, and clubs to develop diverse interests and talents; and Community Engagement, where we will foster a sense of community and social responsibility through volunteer work, community service, and partnerships with local organizations.

FACILITIES

School Bus

The school provides a safe and reliable bus facility with experienced drivers and GPS tracking. This facility is available for students of all grades, making it convenient for parents.

Science Lab

Our science lab fosters curiosity and experimentation with hands-on learning in various scientific disciplines. The lab is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment, chemicals, and specimens for safe experimentation.

Computer Lab

Our computer lab is equipped with the latest technology and software for hands-on learning. Experienced teachers guide students in learning programming languages, software applications, and digital skills.


Library

The school's library is a quiet and comfortable space with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources. It fosters a love of reading and learning with a wide range of fiction and non-fiction books.

Play Ground

The school's playground is a spacious and well-maintained area for physical activities. It features various sports facilities, including a basketball court, football field, and cricket pitch.

Smart Classes

Our smart classes use the latest digital technology, including interactive whiteboards and educational software. These classes provide an engaging and immersive learning experience for students.
